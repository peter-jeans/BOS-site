AI Caveats

Last updated: 18 June 2026.

BOS is advisory

BOS can help route, structure, scan, and verify AI-assisted development work, but AI and governance advice can still be incomplete, stale, overbroad, or wrong.

What receipts mean

  • Receipts record what BOS attempted, what evidence was available, and what verification was performed.
  • Receipts do not prove legal compliance, security certification, accessibility certification, production readiness, or business fitness.

Required human review

  • Review code changes before release.
  • Run platform-appropriate tests and manual checks.
  • Use qualified legal, security, privacy, and financial advice when needed.
  • Treat BOS blocks, soft blocks, and backup warnings as operational safety signals.
