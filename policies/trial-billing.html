Trial and Billing

Last updated: 18 June 2026.

Beta pricing hypothesis

  • Beta Builder: proposed $39/month.
  • Beta Studio: proposed $149/month.

Future services such as guided repair sessions or formal hardening audits are not part of the current beta billing offer. If introduced later, they should have separate scope, owner approval, deliverables, receipts, pricing, and cancellation/refund terms.

Trial posture

BOS may offer a limited trial or onboarding review before paid access. Any trial should clearly state duration, included capabilities, conversion rules, and cancellation process before checkout.

Billing implementation

  • Payment processing should be handled by a payment provider such as Stripe.
  • Cloud BOS should decide entitlement from verified backend events, not from browser state or client claims.
  • Base44 apps should never receive payment-provider secrets or Cloudflare admin tokens.

Plan limits

Capabilities may be limited by plan, app count, scan count, support level, rate limits, advisory catalogue access, or beta availability.
