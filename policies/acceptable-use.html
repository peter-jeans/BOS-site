Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026.

Allowed use

BOS may be used to improve reliability, maintainability, governance, prompt quality, platform fit, and repair workflows for software projects you are authorized to build or maintain.

Prohibited use

  • Building malware, credential theft, unauthorized scraping, exploit automation, evasion tooling, or systems intended to harm people or services.
  • Submitting secrets, passwords, private keys, regulated personal data, payment data, or third-party confidential material to Cloud BOS.
  • Using BOS to bypass platform rules, subscription controls, rate limits, identity checks, or access restrictions.
  • Attempting to extract private BOS internals, non-public Cloud BOS code, tokens, or another user's project data.
  • Using BOS for unauthorized surveillance, privacy-invasive tracking, or unlawful activity.

Enforcement

BOS may block, throttle, suspend, or reject requests that appear unsafe, unauthorized, abusive, or outside the beta scope.
