Refund and Cancellation

Last updated: 18 June 2026.

Cancellation

Users should be able to cancel future subscription renewal through the billing provider or by contacting BOS support. Cancellation should stop future charges but may not automatically refund prior paid periods.

Refund posture

  • Beta refund terms should be short, visible, and confirmed before checkout.
  • One-off audits may be refundable before work begins, partially refundable after work starts, and non-refundable after delivery unless required by law or agreed otherwise.
  • Subscription refunds should account for service availability, billing error, duplicate charge, and local consumer law requirements.

Access after cancellation

Cloud BOS may downgrade entitlement, stop paid capabilities, or keep read-only receipts and public materials available depending on plan and policy.
